About Twatch

Twatch is a website intended to provide a platform for reporting and viewing ongoing emergencies in Nigeria such as Boko Haram attacks, kidnapping and Banditry. The creators of Twatch hope that a real time website with such time critical updates would help save numerous lives in Nigeria and any conflict stricken zones in the world.

Where

Nigeria and any conflict stricken zone in the world.

When

Today, Tomorrow and as long as violent conflicts exist.

Twatch was created by a group of Vanderbilt students during VandyHacks IX (Ocean Edition). We hope that Twatch can be adopted all over the world
